# Overview
In Abrahamic religions, Sodom and Gomorrah were two cities destroyed by god for their wickedness. 

## Lot 
God sends two angels to destroy Sodom, whom Lot welcomes into his home. All the men of the town demand the two visitors are surrendered so they may "Know" them carnally. Lot instead offers his virgin daughters to the men, who refuse. the angels then strike the crowd blind.
