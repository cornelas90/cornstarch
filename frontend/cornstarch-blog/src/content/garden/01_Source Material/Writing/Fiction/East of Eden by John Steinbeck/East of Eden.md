---
**Author:** [[John Steinbeck]]
**Year Published:** 1952
**Genre:** Family Saga, Novel
**Synopsis:** Written by [[John Steinbeck]] in 1952, East of Eden follows multiple generations of two families living in Salinas, CA. Thematically, the story focuses on the human condition and its relationship to good and evil, faith, and impermanence.
