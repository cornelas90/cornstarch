**Kayak angst** (Danish: *kajaksvimmelhed* "kayak dizziness" or **kajakangst**, Greenlandic: *nangiarneq*) or **nangierneq** ([Inuit languages](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Inuit_languages "Inuit languages")) is a condition likened to a [panic attack](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Panic_attack "Panic attack") which has historically been associated with the [Greenlandic Inuit](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Greenlandic_Inuit "Greenlandic Inuit"). It has specifically been described as an episode of intense anxiety amongst [seal hunters](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Seal_hunting "Seal hunting") fishing in [kayaks](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Kayak "Kayak") or similar one-man boats.<sup><a href="https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/#cite_note-Jackson2006-1"><span>[</span>1<span>]</span></a></sup> It has additionally been associated with the [Igloolik](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Igloolik "Igloolik") Inuit of Northern Canada, who are said to suffer wild hallucinations of mythical spirits including visions of a 'sea [ermine](https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/Stoat "Stoat") '.<sup><a href="https://en.m.wikipedia.org/wiki/#cite_note-Merkur1992-2"><span>[</span>2<span>]</span></a></sup>
